Frequently Asked Questions

Can I upgrade my plan later?

Yes, you can upgrade to a higher plan anytime. You only pay the difference in price.

What is the scratch card option?

Scratch cards offer discounted rates and can be purchased from authorized dealers nationwide.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept bank transfers, mobile money (Paystack, Flutterwave), and scratch card redemption.

Is my data secure?

Yes, we use enterprise-grade security with SSL encryption. Data is hosted on trusted cloud infrastructure with AWS (US) and Microsoft Azure (UK) for maximum reliability and compliance with international standards.

Where is our data hosted?

We partner with tier-1 hosting providers including Amazon Web Services (AWS) in the US and Microsoft Azure in the UK, ensuring 99.9% uptime, enterprise-grade security, and GDPR compliance.

Do you offer local Nigerian support?

Yes, while our infrastructure is hosted on global platforms for reliability, we maintain local Nigerian support teams and comply with all local data protection regulations.

What about data backup and recovery?

We maintain automated daily backups across multiple geographic regions (US East, UK West) with 30-day retention and disaster recovery protocols meeting international standards.
